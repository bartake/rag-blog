18. Enterprise Use Cases

Customer support, knowledge assistants, document intelligence, search

Customer Support Copilots

RAG powers chatbots that answer from internal KB, policies, FAQs. Agents get suggested answers with sources. Reduces escalations and improves resolution time.

Internal Knowledge Assistants

Workers ask questions over Confluence, SharePoint, wikis. RAG surfaces relevant docs, SOPs, past decisions. Critical for onboarding and cross-team knowledge.

Document Intelligence

Extract answers from contracts, reports, compliance docs. RAG + chunking handles long docs. Use metadata for filtering (date, department, doc type).

AI-Powered Search Platforms

Semantic search over product catalogs, support tickets, research. Combines keyword + vector. Enables natural-language discovery.
