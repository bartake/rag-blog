3. Vector Databases Fundamentals

Storing and querying embeddings at scale

Embeddings & Similarity
Embeddings in Practice
768–1536 dim float arrays per chunk. Stored in vector DB; query embedded & compared at retrieval time.
Cosine Similarity
Angle between vectors. Range [-1,1]. Use when magnitude doesn't matter.
L2 & Dot Product
L2 = Euclidean distance. Dot product (normalized ≈ cosine). Pick per embedding model.
Indexing
Exact Search
Brute-force O(n). Accurate but slow at scale.
ANN
HNSW, IVF, LSH. Sublinear, 95–99% recall. Right choice for most RAG.
Tools
Pinecone
Managed, serverless. Simple API, auto-scaling.
Weaviate
Open-source, GraphQL, hybrid search, built-in vectorizers.
Milvus
Distributed, billion-scale. For high-throughput enterprises.
Metadata Filtering
Filter by doc_id, language, date. Pre/post/hybrid. Critical for multi-tenant RAG.
