2. Information Retrieval Fundamentals

Keyword search, metrics, and relevance

Search Types
Keyword Search
Matches exact/stemming terms. Fast & interpretable but fails on synonyms. "run" → "running".
Semantic Search
Uses embeddings for meaningful similarity. "fix flat tire" can match "puncture repair" with no word overlap.
TF-IDF & BM25
TF-IDF
TF × IDF. Rewards frequent terms in doc; penalizes terms in many docs. Good baseline for sparse retrieval.
BM25
Improves TF-IDF: saturates term freq, doc-length normalization. Go-to for keyword retrieval.
Metrics
Precision
Relevant Retrieved / Retrieved. Fewer false positives.
Recall
Relevant Retrieved / All Relevant. Fewer false negatives.
F1
Harmonic mean of precision & recall. In RAG: recall for context, precision for clean window.
Ranking
NDCG accounts for position. Top results matter more. Retrieve top-K, optionally re-rank.
