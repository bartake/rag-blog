5. Data Preparation & Ingestion

Getting your documents ready for retrieval

Document Parsing
PDF
PyMuPDF, pdfplumber, Unstructured. Tables: Tabula, Camelot.
HTML
BeautifulSoup, Trafilatura. Extract main content, strip nav/footer.
Structured (JSON/CSV)
Map fields to text. Flatten or template for consistent chunks.
Chunking Strategies
Fixed
By char/token count (e.g. 512). Simple but can break mid-paragraph.
Overlapping
Sliding window + overlap. Reduces boundary loss; slightly more storage.
Semantic
Paragraphs/sections or embedding-based. Preserves meaning; variable size.
Cleaning & Metadata
Cleaning
Remove boilerplate, normalize whitespace/encoding. Better embedding quality.
Metadata Design
source, url, title, section, page, date. Filtering + citation.
