12. Scaling RAG Systems

Index sharding, distributed search, caching, latency, streaming

Scale & Performance
Index Sharding
Split by tenant, time, category. Merge & re-rank. Per-tenant scaling.
Distributed Vector Search
Milvus, Weaviate cluster. Replication for reads; sharding for writes.
Caching
Embeddings + LLM responses. Redis/Memcached, TTLs. High hit rates for FAQs.
Latency
Batch embeddings, async pipelines, connection pooling. Pre-compute at ingest.
Streaming
SSE/WebSocket for token streaming. Retrieval before stream starts.
