Retrieval Metrics

Recall@K

Of all relevant documents, what fraction appear in the top-K results? Critical for RAG—low recall means the LLM never sees key context.

Precision@K

Of the top-K retrieved documents, how many are relevant? High precision keeps the context window focused.

End-to-End Evaluation

Answer Correctness

Does the generated answer match the ground truth? Use exact match, BLEU, or LLM-as-judge for semantic similarity.

Faithfulness

Is the answer grounded in the retrieved context? Detects hallucination. Use NLI models or LLM judges to check claim attribution.

Human vs Automated Evaluation

Automated metrics are scalable but imperfect. Human evaluation on a sample captures nuance.
