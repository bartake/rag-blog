6. Embedding Strategy

Choosing and optimizing embedding models for RAG

Models & Tradeoffs
Choosing Models
OpenAI, Cohere, sentence-transformers, E5, BGE. Consider: dimensions, languages, max length, cost, on-prem.
Cost vs Accuracy
1536-dim vs 384-dim. Benchmark on your domain; smaller often rivals larger.
Domain-Specific
BioBERT (medical), CodeBERT (code). Use when generic underperforms.
Batch vs Real-Time
Batch: pre-embed at ingest. Real-time: embed query only. ~50–200ms per query.
